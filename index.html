Our Story

Welcome to Artem Clava India — a creative house where art, design, and craftsmanship converge to redefine modern interiors and artistic education.

From handcrafted wall murals that replace wallpapers with timeless beauty, to our Fine Arts and Digital Art Academy shaping the next generation of artists, Artem Clava stands at the intersection of luxury and accessibility.

We transform blank walls into stories, nurture creative minds into professionals, and create meaningful art experiences that connect emotion, space, and imagination.

At Artem Clava, every brushstroke has purpose — and every creation carries a soul.

Mission

To provide accessible luxury art through handcrafted design, empower artists and learners through world-class training, and build a sustainable art ecosystem where creativity thrives across all forms.

Vision

To redefine the role of art in modern life — where every wall, mind, and heart becomes a canvas for expression, emotion, and evolution.

Art Therapy & Counseling Program

At Artem Clava, we believe that art is a language of healing. Our Art Therapy Course combines the science of psychology with the soul of creativity — helping individuals heal, express, and evolve.

Program Modules:

  • Fundamentals of Art Therapy
  • Colour Psychology & Emotion
  • Expressive and Therapeutic Art Techniques

  • Art as a Counseling Tool
