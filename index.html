About Our Art School

Where creativity meets mindfulness

Artem Clava is a premier art institution in Chennai dedicated to nurturing artistic talent through comprehensive education, curated exhibitions, and therapeutic art practices.

Founded on the principles of creative expression and mindful healing, our school offers a unique blend of traditional techniques and contemporary approaches to art.

  • Professional art instruction
  • Masterclasses with renowned artists
  • Art therapy and counseling
  • Certificate and diploma programs

Our Services

Comprehensive art education and therapy

Modern Art Instruction

Learn contemporary art techniques from experienced instructors in various mediums including acrylic, oil, watercolor, and mixed media.

Masterclasses

Exclusive workshops with renowned artists covering specialized techniques, art theory, and professional practices.

Art Therapy

Healing through creative expression with certified art therapists for stress relief, emotional growth, and mindfulness.

Premium Education

Structured programs for all skill levels, from beginner courses to advanced diploma certifications in fine arts.

Art Consulting

Professional guidance for art collectors, interior designers, and corporate clients in art selection and curation.

Workshops & Events
