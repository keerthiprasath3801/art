Our Story

Founded in 2020 by psychologist and passionate artist Gia Balan, Artem Clava was born from a desire to break away from rigid, traditional art education. With a focus on healing, inclusion, and creativity, the center grew into a safe and vibrant space for students of all ages to learn, express, and transform through art.

Mission

To bridge the worlds of art and mental wellness, empowering students through creative expression and emotional healing.

Vision

We envision a world where art is celebrated as a path to emotional well-being and empowerment.
